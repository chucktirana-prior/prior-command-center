# Uploader Authoring Rules

These are the safest rules for anyone preparing a document for the Prior uploader.

## Required formatting rules

1. Keep each label on its own line.
2. Use the exact label names from the template.
3. Put `Keep reading:` on its own line if you use it.
4. Put `Article body:` on its own line.
5. Start the article body on the line after `Article body:`.
6. Keep `Keywords:` as one comma-separated line if possible.
7. Keep `Slug:` on its own line.
8. Keep `Hero caption:` on its own line.

## Avoid these mistakes

- Do not combine `Keywords:` and `Slug:` on one line.
- Do not combine `Social:` with any other label.
- Do not put article copy on the same line as `Email intro text`, `Social`, or `Keywords`.
- Do not rely on `Keep reading:` as the start of the article body.
- Do not place body copy before `Article body:`.
- Do not improvise new label names if the existing ones will work.
